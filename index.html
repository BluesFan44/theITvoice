My Background

Ever since I had my first video game system, I've been a typical computer geek. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have been working in the IT field in various capacities since 1992 at companies such as Monsanto, Anheuser-Busch, and Boeing. I build my own computers, write my own database scripts, and set up my own websites - including this one!

My first passion in life has always been about helping people, and IT has always been about helping people do things faster, easier and more accurately.

My second passion is the St. Louis Blues hockey team and all things NHL.
